In a world where Goku from Dragon Ball Z teams up with Naruto from Naruto Shippuden to tackle a cosmic threat, they find themselves battling alongside Sailor Moon from Sailor Moon and Edward Elric from Fullmetal Alchemist. As they confront an otherworldly villain reminiscent of Attack on Titan, their combined powers and strategies prove crucial. Meanwhile, Luffy from One Piece and Spike Spiegel from Cowboy Bebop navigate through chaotic space battles, ensuring that the fate of their intertwined universes hangs in the balance.
